About the Author

Hi, I’m Priyanshu. I’m a student who writes to understand life a little better. Sometimes I observe small things — like a memory, or a quiet moment — and try to see what they are really teaching me. I’m not here to be perfect. I’m just here to express what I feel and how I see the world. This space is my journal — a place for reflection, growth, and honest thoughts. Thank you for reading.
